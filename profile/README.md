# Melvora Studio

We are an independent product studio building modern software products.

Our focus is on simple, useful and well-designed tools for small businesses, local services and everyday operations.

## Current products

### Resviqo

Resviqo is our first SaaS product for local service businesses.

It helps salons, studios, trainers and other local providers create a public booking link, show services and prices, manage availability and receive online reservations.

## Repositories

* `resviqo-web` — public marketing website for Resviqo
* `resviqo-app` — SaaS application and product development

## How we work

We build in small steps, keep the product simple and focus on real customer problems first.

Current focus:

* launch the Resviqo marketing website
* validate the offer with first customers
* prepare the foundation for the SaaS application

## Status

Melvora Studio is currently in early product development.
